Character

The main character is designed as an ordinary, slim-build individual with a slightly hunched posture to emphasize nervousness, vulnerability and curiosity. His casual clothing and simplistic appearance help to ground the story in realism, making the character more relatable.

The flashlight in his hands serves as both a narrative tool and a visual one, guiding attention while reinforcing the suspenseful tone.

Body language focuses on hesitation, curiosity, and fear. Facial features such as tired eyes and subtle expressions were explored to suggest anxiety without exaggeration.

Environment

The mansion exterior was designed to appear imposing and isolated. The interior corridor emphasizes narrow proportions, locked doors, and low lighting to enhance suspense and guide the viewer’s focus.
